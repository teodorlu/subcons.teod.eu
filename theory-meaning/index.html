A theory of meaning

  1. Have a worthy persuit
  2. Take care of your friends
  3. Stay true to your priciples

I'm not certain about the last one, it smells like it only applies to me. I've got principles. Other people have a sense of right and wrong. And I don't think that everyone should go around defining principles. What I do think, however, is that when we stay in line with what we presume is right, that leaves us better for it.
